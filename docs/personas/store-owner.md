# Persona: E-Commerce Store Owner

## Profile
- **Name:** Sarah Chen
- **Role:** Founder & owner of a mid-size D2C brand (home goods)
- **Age:** 38
- **Tech comfort:** Moderate — comfortable with SaaS tools, not a developer
- **Team size:** 5 people (herself, 2 warehouse, 1 marketing, 1 CS)
- **Revenue:** $1.2M/year, growing 30% YoY
- **Time in Maropost:** 2–4 hours/day

## Daily Tasks in Maropost (by frequency)

1. **Check dashboard** — revenue, order count, open rate trends (every morning)
2. **Process orders** — review new orders, approve for fulfillment, handle exceptions (2–3x/day)
3. **Fulfillment** — print packing slips, mark shipped, enter tracking numbers (daily)
4. **Product management** — update inventory, adjust prices, add new SKUs (weekly)
5. **Review campaign performance** — open rates, click rates, revenue attribution (weekly)
6. **Draft orders** — create manual orders for wholesale/phone orders (occasional)
7. **Coupons** — create promo codes for seasonal sales (monthly)

## Key Pages Used

| Page | Frequency | What she does |
|------|-----------|--------------|
| Dashboard | Daily | Quick health check — revenue, orders, campaigns |
| Sales Orders | Daily | Filter by "Processing", bulk fulfill, handle exceptions |
| Fulfillments | Daily | Ship orders, enter tracking |
| Products | Weekly | Check stock levels, update prices |
| Draft Orders | Occasional | Manual orders for wholesale customers |
| Coupons | Monthly | Create promo codes for sales events |
| Analytics > Sales Summary | Weekly | Revenue trends, top products |

## Pain Points

1. **Too many clicks to fulfill** — wants to select 20 orders → bulk mark as shipped in one action
2. **Can't quickly see which orders need attention** — wants a "Needs Action" filter showing payment issues + unfulfilled orders together
3. **Dashboard doesn't show what matters** — wants revenue, orders, and fulfillment backlog at a glance, not email stats
4. **Product inventory warnings** — wants to see low-stock alerts without navigating to Products
5. **Status columns are confusing** — three separate statuses (order, fulfillment, payment) per order row is overwhelming

## Success Metrics
- Orders fulfilled within 24 hours of payment
- Zero overselling (inventory accuracy)
- Revenue growth visible week-over-week

## Interface Expectations
- Large, clear numbers on the dashboard
- Bulk actions that actually save time
- Status indicators she can scan in 2 seconds
- Mobile-friendly for checking orders on the go
- "Smart defaults" — if 90% of orders go to the same warehouse, pre-select it

## Quote
> "I don't want to spend my day in the admin panel. Show me what needs my attention, let me act on it fast, and get me back to running my business."
